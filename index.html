Why Dayton?

Dayton, the birthplace of aviation

Dayton, Ohio is situated in the heart of the Midwest region of the United States, is a bustling and lively metropolis. As the county seat of Montgomery County, it boasts a rich and fascinating history, dating back to the early days of the nation. Dayton is renowned as the "Birthplace of Aviation," as it was here that the Wright brothers made their groundbreaking invention of the airplane in the early 1900s.

This city has a diverse population that provides numerous cultural, educational, and recreational opportunities. Visitors can explore various museums, including the National Museum of the United States Air Force, which showcases a vast collection of aircraft and aviation artifacts. Dayton also boasts a lively arts and entertainment scene, with theaters, music venues, and festivals catering to diverse interests. Additionally, the city is dedicated to research and technology, with numerous research institutions and manufacturing facilities. Dayton is a vibrant community that artfully blends its historical significance with modern amenities.
